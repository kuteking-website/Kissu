About Kissu

Kissu is a Telegram-powered dating platform designed to help people build genuine relationships in a secure and engaging environment.

Our mission is to make meeting new people simple, safe and enjoyable by combining smart matching, modern technology and a privacy-first approach.

Users can create profiles, discover new people, match, chat securely, subscribe to VIP features and receive support directly through Telegram.

We continuously improve Kissu by introducing new features focused on user safety, better matching and an enjoyable experience.
